All About Me: Ashli Green


My name is Ashli Green. I am second year student at the Univeristy of Georgia. I am currently an Intended Business Management and International Business Major. I also plan on earning a Certificate in Music Business and a Minor in German. I'll be using this website to tell you about me; including my interests, organizations I am in, and what I plan to do after I graduate from UGA.

Background Info

I was born in Tupelo, MS on July 31,1997. My father served in the US Army and I spent most of my childhood overseas. I lived in Germany from 2001-2011. I started high school in Augusta, GA at Lucy C Laney High School. In high school I participated in several after school activities. For example, I participated in Student Council. I also played soccer and was in the marching band. I graduated from Lucy C Laney as valedictorian of my class.

Hobbies

I have quite a few hobbies. Some of which include playing music, traveling, art, makeup, and reading. I started playing the alto saxophone in 9th grade and this is the main instrument that I play. I have also learned how to play the keyboard but I am still a beginner and I am currently learning how to play the tuba. I enjoy watching Youtube videos of college bands and their dance teams. My absolute favorite college band is the Human Jukebox from Southern Universityvideo. Some of my other favorite bands are the Sonic Boom of the South from Jackson State University and the Sounds of Dyn-O-mite from Alcorn State University. I also enjoy watching videos on Youtube about art and makeup. I would conisder myself a beginner in both of these areas but there are a lot of helpful videos on Youtube. I also really enjoy traveling. I have been visited the majority of the countries in Europe. I enjoy being able to experince other cultures, see new sites, and try new foods. Next I hope to continue to travel the US and then travel the Carribean, Asia, and South America.
